About Shivam Floral Stylist

Welcome to Shivam Floral Stylist, your trusted partner for exquisite floral arrangements and bespoke event décor. We’re passionate about transforming life’s most special moments into beautiful, unforgettable memories.

Our Story

Founded with a deep love for flowers and design, Shivam Floral Stylist was born from a vision to make events truly magical. What started as a small floral studio has grown into a premium service loved by clients for our creativity, quality, and personal touch.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To bring your vision to life with exceptional floral designs and event styling that reflect your unique story. We believe every celebration deserves artistry, attention to detail, and heartfelt service.

What We Offer

  • Wedding décor and floral design
  • Engagement and anniversary celebrations
  • Corporate events and launches
  • Premium floral delivery for special occasions
  • Customized, theme-based event styling

Whether you dream of a classic romantic setting or a bold, modern design, our team will make it happen.

Our Values

  • Quality: Using only the freshest, highest-quality blooms
  • Creativity: Designing unique, tailor-made arrangements
  • Personalization: Understanding your vision and bringing it to life
  • Service: Making every client feel valued and heard

Meet Our Team

Led by Shivam Floral Stylist, our team of talented floral designers and event stylists shares one goal: making your celebration extraordinary. We bring years of experience, artistic flair, and meticulous planning to every event we design.

Our Experience

  • 2000+ successful events styled
  • Years of proven expertise in floral and event design
  • Trusted by couples, families, and corporate clients across the region

Our work has adorned intimate weddings, grand receptions, elegant corporate launches, and everything in between.
